# European AI Infrastructure Map

Public map of European AI compute, programs, AI Factories, funding paths, university labs, research centers, open source communities, and startups.

Limes Labs is early. This repository is the first technical artifact: a living document of what already exists in Europe, what is missing, and where contributors can help.

## What we are mapping

- EuroHPC access paths and supercomputing sites
- AI Factories network and national Antennas
- IT4LIA and Italian AI infrastructure (Leonardo, LISA, GAIA, MEGARIDE)
- University labs and research centers
- European open source AI groups
- Funding programs and application requirements
- European AI startups working on sovereign or open models
- Legal and institutional constraints

## Status

Phase I — infrastructure and people. We are researching and starting outreach. We are not claiming compute access yet.

## How to contribute

1. Open an issue with a program, lab, startup, or access path we should document.
2. Submit a pull request with structured notes and sources.
3. Join the contributor network at [limeslabs.eu/join](https://limeslabs.eu/join).
